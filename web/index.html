Call for Papers

We invite you to submit your talks to be part of the conference:

Submit your papers here

A talk duration could range between 15-45 minutes, and it can be in a variety of angular related topics (performance, debugging, angular 2.0, specific package / bug / issue you ran into), anything others can learn from.

If you are not sure, just talk to us ngconf@gdg.co.il. We'll be happy to assist in choosing the exact topic or helping you decide whether your idea fits the conference spirit. Our community is ever expanding and we welcome new members to join and suggest ideas.

The call for papers deadline is end of Apr 21. To make sure every application gets the attention it deserves, please submit on time.
